About Me

Hi This is SRIDHARAN S, I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology. I am a tech enthusiast with a keen interest in cutting-edge technologies and their applications. My passion lies in the domain of VLSI design, where I aspire to build a strong career contributing to innovative semiconductor technologies. Being an active learner, I enjoy exploring topics like digital design, system-on-chip architecture, and hardware acceleration for AI.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I actively participate in technical workshops and hackathons to enhance my skill set. I am a member of IEEE SRM SB RAS Domain, where I collaborate on projects and engage in knowledge-sharing activities. My ultimate goal is to excel as a VLSI engineer, combining creativity and technical expertise to make a significant impact in the electronics industry.

My Journey

My educational journey began at Velammal Vidyashram, Mambakkam, where I developed a strong foundation in academics and a passion for computer science. In my 12th grade, I scored an impressive 94% in Computer Science, showcasing my dedication and perseverance.

During this time, I cultivated a deep interest in electronics and chip designing, which later became the cornerstone of my aspirations. This passion drove me to pursue Electronics and Communication Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, where I continue to expand my knowledge and skills in the field.
